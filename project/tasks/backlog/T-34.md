# Add crew training or reassign role

**Type:** feature
**Labels:** feature, crew
**Effort:** 2 points
**Status:** inbox

## Description
Spend coins to "retrain" a crew from one role to another. Lets you correct your mix without losing crew.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Player can retrain a crew member to a different role (miner, scientist, medic, pilot) at a coin cost.
- [ ] Crew count is unchanged; only role changes.
- [ ] Cost and UI are clear.

## Dependencies
None
